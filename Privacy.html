Privacy Policy for Fixit Last Updated: January 17, 2026 At Fixit, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. 1. Information We Collect Images and Visual Data: To provide our core service (AI-powered repair analysis), we process photos you take within the app or upload from your library. Location Data: We collect information about your geographical location to connect you with local professionals. Usage Data: We collect anonymous information about how you interact with the app (e.g., which rooms are scanned most often) to improve our AI models. Device Information: We may collect data such as your device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers to ensure app stability. 2. App Permissions and Usage To provide a full range of features, Fixit requests access to certain device functions. Below is how and why we use these permissions: Camera (NSCameraUsageDescription): Fixit uses your camera to capture photos of home repairs, damage, or issues. For example, you can take a photo of a water leak or cracked tile, and Fixit will analyze the image to provide diagnostic information, repair guidance, and cost estimates. Location (NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription): Fixit uses your location to find nearby service providers and contractors in your area when you request to search for local repair services. Photo Library (NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription): Fixit uses your photo library to upload existing photos of home repairs or damage for diagnosis and repair guidance. 3. How We Use Your Information We use the information collected to: Analyze home repair issues and provide cost estimates. Generate DIY instructions tailored to your specific problem. Find nearby service providers based on your location. Improve our AI algorithms for better accuracy. Monitor and analyze usage trends. 4. AI Processing and Data Retention Image Analysis: Photos are uploaded to our secure servers for processing via specialized AI models. Retention: We do not store your repair photos indefinitely. Images are typically deleted from our active servers after the analysis report is generated unless you specifically choose to save them to your account. No Sale of Data: We do not sell your photos or personal data to third parties. 5. Third-Party Service Providers We may use third-party companies (such as OpenAI, Google Cloud, or AWS) to facilitate our AI analysis. These providers have access to your information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose. 6. Your Rights and Choices You have the right to: Delete your app data at any time through the in-app settings. Revoke app permissions (Camera, Location, Photos) at any time through your device settings. Opt-out of anonymous analytics tracking. Request a copy of the data we have associated with your account. 7. Contact Us If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: Ztzmarketing@gmail.com
